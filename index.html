Usage Of Blasting Mat

Blasting mats are used when the explosives are detonate at in place like construction sites.The Blasting mat is placed over the blastng to reduce the noise of explosive and reduce the pollution and prevent Rock Flying.The blast mats provided optimal protection and greatly increase the safety of site workers and public. The Blasting mat is Easily hanging up and placed over the blasting are using the Crane.The mayor advantage of blasting mat is is the fact that during explosion the mat cover allows the air pressure and gases through while itself does not move (or hardly) but still containers fly rocks and debris.
